Proof of Linear Independence Theorem

The Linear Independence Theorem (it may have other names) states that all linearly independent lists of vectors in a Vector Space have a length less than or equal to the length any spanning list of the same vector space. Thousands of textbooks have proved this statement and the subsequent proof is great.
