About

This is what I do.

This website has no discernible direction. It is a place for the average college student to talk about something that bothers, interests, or excites them. It is for the college student looking to educate others, it is for the college student who wants to organize his or her thoughts, it is for the the college student who needs a platform to talk about something no one will listen too. No Agendas, No Motives, just Pure Unmolested Ideas.

Even as a regular kid, I go through a vast amount of rich mental and physical experiences, so I vehemently refuse to believe that some people can be categorized as chronically boring or one dimensional. We all have something to say about something, whether it be about arranged marriage, fractal geometry, or sports analytics.

I want to hear about it
